Andrea Voulgaris - Saxophone and Voice

Bio

Andrea Voulgaris is an active performer and instructor of saxophone, residing in southeast Michigan. She holds a Bachelor of Music Performance degree from Grand Valley State University as well as a Masters in Music Performance degree and a Specialist in Music Performance degree from University of Michigan School of Music, Theater, and Dance.

Performances

"La Virgen de la Macarena" by Raphael Mendez
"The Trouble with the Wind" by Evan Chambers
